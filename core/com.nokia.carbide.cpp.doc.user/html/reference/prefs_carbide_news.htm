Carbide.c++ News preference panel

The Carbide.c++ News preferences panel enables you to control the behavior of the Carbide.c++ News view.

Figure 1. Carbide.c++ News preference panel

Table 1 Carbide News preference panel —items
Item Explanation
Display Carbide.c++ news page at startup

Enable to always show the news view when Carbide is launched. Recommended to ensure you are kept aware of the latest Carbide news and related information. Disable to not show the page on launch.
Subscribed news feeds The list of subscribled news feeds.
Update news feed Select the time period that the news reader checks for updated stories.
